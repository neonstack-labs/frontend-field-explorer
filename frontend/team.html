Meet Engr. Ijilusi Adeniyi O., a dynamic and accomplished leader who took the helm as Chief Executive Officer in 2014, marking a new era of re-organisation and full operation for Field Explorer Limited. A COREN-certified Civil Engineer, his engineering journey began in 2011 at ASBValiant Co. Ltd where his exceptional achievements led to his selection to manage and oversee all company operations.

Throughout his career, Adeniyi has honed his skills as a Civil and Structural Designer, Project Manager and Site Manager — including expertise in power plant construction. Driven by a thirst for knowledge, he has attended numerous seminars, workshops, and international training programs, staying ahead of the curve in the ever-evolving field of engineering.
